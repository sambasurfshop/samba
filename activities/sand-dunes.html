Why choose this lesson?

Pure adrenaline on golden dunes.

LESSON ROADMAP

Meet & Gear Up

Meet your instructor at the skate park or your hotel if transportation is included. Get your skate or surf skate ready and fit your protective gear (helmets and pads are recommended).

Warm-Up & Basics

Start with a warm-up and basic techniques:

  • Balance and stance
  • Pushing and turning
  • Safety and park etiquette

Guided Practice

Practice on the park under instructor supervision. Beginners learn foundational moves while advanced skaters refine tricks and flow.

Free Skate Time

After the guided session, enjoy some independent practice to apply your new skills and have fun in the park.

Wrap-Up & Photos

End the session with tips for improvement and optional photos to remember your experience.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Do I need experience?

Not at all! Sandboarding is easy to learn, and beginners get a quick orientation before starting.

What should I bring?

Just:

  • Comfortable clothes for movement
  • Water bottle
  • Optional: your own helmet/pads (we provide rentals)

We provide board + wetsuit + leash + wax.

Is sandboarding safe?

Yes — it’s low risk and done on soft sand. Your guide will assist throughout the activity.
