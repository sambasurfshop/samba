samba surf story

Samba Surf Shop was created from a love for the ocean and the joy of sharing unforgettable surf moments. For us, surfing is more than a sport it’s a feeling, a lifestyle, and a way to connect with people from all around the world.

Our team of passionate surfers and instructors is dedicated to helping everyone catch their first wave, improve their skills, and enjoy the beauty of the beach.

We welcome beginners, travelers, and advanced surfers with the same warm Samba energy.

Every lesson we give, every board we rent, and every experience we offer is guided by our commitment to fun, safety, and positive vibes.

Join the Samba family and discover what surfing truly means.

Why Taghazout?

Located on Morocco’s beautiful Atlantic coastline, our beach offers the ideal mix of smooth beginner waves, powerful breaks for advanced riders, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. It’s the perfect place to learn, explore, and enjoy unforgettable surf moments.

Consistent Waves

Enjoy reliable swells and surf-friendly conditions throughout the year perfect for every level.

Sunshine Almost Every Day

More than 300 days of sunshine make every session warm, bright, and full of good vibes.

Real Moroccan Vibes

Experience local hospitality, rich culture, and the authentic charm of our coastal village.

Friendly Surf Community

Meet new friends, share waves, and feel at home with surfers from all over the world.
