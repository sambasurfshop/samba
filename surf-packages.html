Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get ready for your surf adventure.

Do I need any surfing experience?

Not at all! Our surf lessons are beginner-friendly and designed for all levels. Whether it’s your first wave or your fiftieth, we’ve got you covered.

What is included?

All surf equipment is included: board, wetsuit, and leash, plus experienced Surf instructors.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing a towel, sunscreen, swimwear, and a positive attitude!

How long is each surf lesson?

Each surf lesson lasts approximately 2 hours, including warm-up, theory, and in-water practice.

How do I schedule?

You can book online through our website or contact us directly for availability.

Do you provide hotel pickup?

At the moment, hotel pickup is not available. All guests are kindly asked to meet us at our surf shop in Taghazout. We're centrally located and easy to find—feel free to contact us if you need help with directions.

What happens if the weather is bad?

If conditions are unsafe, we’ll reschedule your lesson or offer a full refund. Your safety is always our priority.

Is there an age limit?

Our lessons are suitable for all ages 6 and above. Kids need to be accompanied by an adult.

Can I rent equipment?

Yes, we offer equipment rentals for both beginners and experienced surfers.

Are other activities available?

In addition to surf lessons, we also offer activities like desert trips, sandboarding, skateboarding lessons, stand-up paddleboarding, horse riding and more. Check out our Activities page.

Still have questions?

Contact us or visit our shop for further assistance.
